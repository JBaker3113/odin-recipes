Homemade Biscuits

Description

Ingredients

  • 2 cups all purpose flour
  • 1 Tbsp baking powder
  • 1 Tbsp sugar
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 6 Tbsp unsalted butter
  • 3/4 cup whole milk

Instructions

  1. Freeze butter for 20 minutes before starting
  2. Preheat oven to 425F and line cookie sheet with parchment
  3. Combine flour,sugar, salt and baking powder in large bowl and mix
  4. Remove butter from freezer and grate in to flour mixture and stir
  5. Continue to mix until texture is similar to coarse crumbs
  6. Add milk and stir with wooden spoon or spatula until combined
  7. Transfer dough to well floured surface and work dough together with hands (If dough too sticky add flour until manageable
  8. Once dough is cohesive flatten dough and fold it over on itself, turn dough 90 degrees and fold again. Repeated 5 to 6 times
  9. Using your hands flatten to 1in thick and cut with lightly dusted 2 3/4 in round biscuit cutter
  10. Once you have cut as many as possible rework dough and repeated until all dough used
  11. Place on cookie sheet no more than 1/2 in apart and bake for 12 minutes
  12. Once finished lightly brush with more butter and serve
